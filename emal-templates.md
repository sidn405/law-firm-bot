## 📝 BEST PRACTICES

### Subject Line Testing
Test these variations:
- Question format: "Davis Crump losing leads after hours?"
- Curiosity: "Your competitor is probably using this already"
- Pain point: "Are your intake staff drowning in leads?"
- Value prop: "24/7 AI intake for Davis Crump?"

### Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 8-10 AM or 2-4 PM (local time)
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

### Follow-Up Cadence
- Day 0: Initial email
- Day 3: Follow-up #1
- Day 7: Follow-up #2
- Day 14: Breakup email

### Personalization Checklist
✅ Use first name (not "Hi there")
✅ Mention their firm name specifically
✅ Reference their practice areas
✅ Use their city/market if relevant
✅ Mention any recent news about their firm

### A/B Testing
Test these variables:
- Short vs. long email
- Question vs. statement subject lines
- Price mentioned vs. price not mentioned
- Demo link at top vs. demo link in middle
- With social proof vs. without

## 💰 PRICING DISCUSSION STRATEGY

**Don't mention price in the first email unless:**
- They're a very large firm (50+ attorneys)
- They specifically asked about pricing
- You're targeting enterprise clients

**When to mention price:**
- During the discovery call (after demonstrating value)
- After they've tried the demo
- When they ask "How much?"

**How to position the price:**
"Investment starts at $25,000 for complete setup and customization. Most firms see ROI within 60-90 days through increased consultations and reduced staffing costs."

**Alternative pricing mention:**
"Our clients typically invest $25K-$35K depending on customization needs. One firm calculated they save $80K annually in intake staff costs alone."
